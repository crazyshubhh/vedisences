About MediChat

MediChat is an innovative medical chatbot designed to assist you in addressing a wide range of health-related queries. Whether you're seeking basic medical advice, reminders for medication, or guidance on finding a doctor, MediChat is your trusted virtual assistant. Accessible 24/7, it bridges the gap between patients and healthcare solutions, ensuring you're never alone in managing your health.

Features of MediChat:

  • Instant health advice for common symptoms and conditions.
  • Medication reminders to help you stay on track.
  • Locate nearby clinics, hospitals, or pharmacies with ease.
  • Basic mental health support and self-care tips.
  • AI-powered responses tailored to your questions.
  • Provides information about health trends and preventative care.
